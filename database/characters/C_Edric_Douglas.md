### Relationship to Logan Douglas

Edric is the biological son of Logan Douglas (PC-012). Logan is his father and primary family authority. Through Logan's establishment (The Verve bar/garage and penthouse), Edric is connected to that location as Logan's son.

### Relationship to Erik Douglas

Edric is the nephew of Erik Douglas. Erik is his paternal uncle (father's brother). Erik is the CEO of DCC and head of the Douglas-Bloodmoon union.

### Relationship to Douglas-Bloodmoon Cousins

Edric is the first cousin of Malachia, Noah, Jasper, and Alyssa Douglas-Bloodmoon. They share a grandfather (the Douglas patriarch) but have different fathers (Logan and Erik are brothers).

**Critical Distinction:** Edric is NOT a sibling of the Douglas-Bloodmoon heirs. He is their cousin. He does NOT carry the Douglas-Bloodmoon hyphenated surname — he carries Douglas.

### Relationship to Nixara Bloodmoon

Edric is the nephew (by marriage) of Nixara Bloodmoon. No blood relation. Nixara is married to Edric's uncle Erik.

### Relationship to Wulfnic Bloodmoon

No blood relation. Wulfnic is the grandfather of Edric's cousins, not Edric's grandfather. Edric's paternal grandfather is the Douglas patriarch.
