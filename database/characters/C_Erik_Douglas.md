## Origin Story — Meeting Nixara

> Canon. Source: Family Authority / ADR-001.

### The Bruins Ranking

Every year, the UCLA Bruins football team and KSA fraternity produce a ranking of the hottest freshmen on campus — scores from 1 to 10. The list circulates out of the Bruins locker rooms, passed around until it reaches the wider campus.

At the time, Erik Douglas was the Bruins quarterback and undisputed field captain — a figure feared and respected across campus.

### Nixara's Score

Nixara Bloodmoon — 5'2", Icelandic-American, ice-blue eyes, platinum blonde — received a perfect 10. She shot to the top of the ranking. The list triggered a wave of gossip about her across campus.

### The Confrontation

Nixara found out about the list. She was furious.

She stormed into the Bruins locker room after a training session while the players — including Erik — were still showering and changing. Without hesitation, she zeroed in on Erik.

The image: this tiny girl screaming up at a 6'9" giant of a quarterback in Icelandic, calling him an *imbecile* and a *codfish head*, while the rest of the team watched in stunned silence.

Erik didn't respond. He didn't shoo her away. He listened.

### Why Erik Fell in Love

Erik — feared quarterback, campus alpha, corporate heir — had never faced anyone with the sheer audacity to confront him like that. Nixara was the first creature in his life who looked at him without an ounce of fear and told him exactly what she thought of him.

That was when he fell in love.

### The Union

Erik and Nixara's relationship developed from that first explosive encounter. Their union — the joining of Douglas and Bloodmoon dynasties — became the foundational event of the Douglas-Bloodmoon family line.

Nixara died in 2005 during childbirth (twins Jasper and Alyssa). Erik never remarried.
