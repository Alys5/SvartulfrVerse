### Relationship to Wulfnic Bloodmoon

Nixara is the daughter of Wulfnic Bloodmoon (PC-001). She was raised by Wulfnic with deep connection to both her Icelandic Bloodmoon heritage and her American identity. Her marriage to Erik Douglas in the 1990s created the Douglas-Bloodmoon dynastic union — the foundational merge of the two family lines.

### Relationship to Erik Douglas

Nixara is the wife of Erik Douglas (MR-001). Their union is the foundational dynastic merge for the Los Angeles Dynasty. The union creates a new dynasty branch (Douglas-Bloodmoon) while preserving the distinct root dynasties.

### Relationship to Children

Nixara is the biological mother of all four Douglas-Bloodmoon heirs:
- **Malachia** (born 1996) — Eldest son, firstborn
- **Noah** (born 1999) — Middle son, second-born
- **Jasper** (born 2005) — Youngest son, twin of Alyssa
- **Alyssa** (born 2005) — Daughter, twin of Jasper

Nixara died during childbirth in 2005, delivering the twins Jasper and Alyssa.

### Relationship to Logan Douglas

Nixara is the sister-in-law of Logan Douglas (Erik's brother). No blood relation.

### Relationship to Edric Douglas

Nixara is the aunt (by marriage) of Edric Douglas (son of Logan). No blood relation.

## Narrative Function

**Primary Role:** Bridge between dynasties. Nixara connects the Bloodmoon line (Icelandic heritage) to the Douglas line (English mercantile tradition) through her union with Erik Douglas.

**Significance:**
- Without Nixara, there is no Douglas-Bloodmoon dynasty.
- She is the maternal template for the first generation's visual identity.
- Her death in 2005 (childbirth) is a foundational family trauma that shapes:
  - Wulfnic's intensified protection of the grandchildren
  - Erik's grief and single parenthood
  - Malachia's vow to protect the family
  - Jasper and Alyssa's maternal absence
