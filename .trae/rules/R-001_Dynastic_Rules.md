# R-001: Dynastic Rules

**Authority:** ADR-001_Dynastic_Origins.md  
**Type:** Operational Rule  
**Date:** 2026-06-07

---

## R-001-DYN-001: Bloodmoon Dynasty Origin

### Authority
ADR-001

### Rule
Bloodmoon Dynasty originates from Iceland, not Norway.

### Rationale
Historical documentation establishes Iceland as the geographic origin. Previous references to Norway are legacy drift.

### Allowed
- Documenting Iceland as Bloodmoon origin
- Referencing Icelandic heritage in character backgrounds
- Querying Family Authority for Bloodmoon dynasty membership

### Prohibited
- Documenting Norway as Bloodmoon origin
- Creating Norwegian heritage references for Bloodmoon characters
- Assuming Nordic = Norwegian

---

## R-001-DYN-002: Bloodmoon Migration Timeline

### Authority
ADR-001

### Rule
Bloodmoon migration to North America occurred after 1930.

### Rationale
The first American-born generation is Wulfnic Bloodmoon. Migration timeline is post-1930, not earlier.

### Allowed
- Documenting post-1930 migration
- Wulfnic Bloodmoon as first American-born
- Contemporary era focus for Bloodmoon in America

### Prohibited
- Pre-1930 American presence for Bloodmoon
- Earlier migration timelines
- Pre-Wulfnic American generations

## R-001-DYN-003: Douglas Dynasty Origin

### Authority
ADR-001

### Rule
Douglas Dynasty originates from England.

### Rationale
Historical documentation establishes England as the geographic origin. Migration to America occurred during 1700s.

### Allowed
- Documenting England as Douglas origin
- Referencing English heritage in character backgrounds
- 1700s migration timeline

### Prohibited
- Alternative origin countries for Douglas
- Earlier or later migration timelines without evidence
- Non-English heritage claims for Douglas line

---

## R-001-DYN-004: Dynasty Distinction

### Authority
ADR-001

### Rule
Bloodmoon and Douglas are distinct dynasties with no pre-union genealogical connection.

### Rationale
The purpose of a dynastic union is to merge two separate family lines. Any pre-union connection would invalidate the architectural concept.

### Allowed
- Documenting separate dynasty origins
- Erik Douglas + Nixara Bloodmoon as union principals
- Douglas-Bloodmoon heirs as first merged generation

### Prohibited
- Pre-union family connections
- Blood relation between Erik and Wulfnic
- Dynasty merge before the union

---

## R-001-DYN-005: Hyphenated Surname Mandate

### Authority
ADR-001

### Rule
All children of Erik Douglas + Nixara Bloodmoon must carry the hyphenated surname "Douglas-Bloodmoon".

### Rationale
The hyphenated surname is a mandatory dynastic designation for the first generation heirs. This is non-negotiable.

### Allowed
- Malachia Douglas-Bloodmoon
- Noah Douglas-Bloodmoon
- Jasper Douglas-Bloodmoon
- Alyssa Douglas-Bloodmoon

### Prohibited
- Malachia Douglas (missing Bloodmoon)
- Malachia Bloodmoon (missing Douglas)
- Any non-hyphenated variant for first generation heirs

---

## R-001-DYN-006: Prohibited Surname Drift

### Authority
ADR-001

### Rule
No surname variations are permitted for Douglas-Bloodmoon first generation heirs.

### Rationale
Historical archives contain documented surname drift. These variations are explicitly rejected as legacy degradation.

### Allowed
- Exact hyphenated form: Douglas-Bloodmoon
- Consistent usage across all references

### Prohibited
- "Douglas Bloodmoon" (missing hyphen)
- "Bloodmoon-Douglas" (wrong order)
- "Douglas" alone
- "Bloodmoon" alone
- Any creative variations

---

## R-001-DYN-007: Wulfnic-Erik Relationship Rejection

### Authority
ADR-001

### Rule
Wulfnic Bloodmoon is NOT the father of Erik Douglas.

### Rationale
This false relationship would make the Erik + Nixara union incestuous (Nixara being Wulfnic's daughter). The relationship is explicitly rejected as legacy drift.

### Allowed
- Wulfnic Bloodmoon as Nixara's father
- Erik Douglas as separate dynasty principal
- No blood relation between Wulfnic and Erik

### Prohibited
- Wulfnic → Erik father-son relationship
- Any genealogical path connecting Wulfnic to Erik
- Documentation suggesting pre-union family connection

---

## R-001-DYN-008: Union Principals

### Authority
ADR-001

### Rule
The Douglas-Bloodmoon union consists of exactly two principals: Erik Douglas and Nixara Bloodmoon.

### Rationale
The dynastic merge is defined by these two individuals. No other union configuration is canonical.

### Allowed
- Erik Douglas as Douglas principal
- Nixara Bloodmoon as Bloodmoon principal
- Four heirs as union result

### Prohibited
- Alternative union configurations
- Additional principals
- Different heir configurations without evidence
