### Context

At the beginning of the academic semester, members of a male fraternity and several UCLA football players create an informal ranking board of incoming freshmen.

**The Board Contains:**
- Attractiveness rankings
- Nicknames for female students

**Nixara Bloodmoon's Position:**
- Placed at the top of the ranking
- The list begins circulating through campus gossip
- Eventually Nixara learns about it

---

## The Incident

### Nixara's Response

Believing the ranking originated with the football team leadership, Nixara marches directly to the UCLA football locker room.

**At the time:**
- Players are changing clothes
- Players are leaving the showers
- Team activities are ongoing

**Nixara's behavior:**
- Ignores the situation entirely
- Her objective: finding the person she believes responsible

**Target:** Erik Douglas — Quarterback and team captain

---

### Recovery Finding

Historical evidence suggests Erik was not actually responsible for creating the ranking.

However, Nixara assumes he is responsible due to his position as team captain.

**This misunderstanding becomes the catalyst for their first interaction.**

## Architectural Finding

### Core Dynamic Establishment

The significance of the event is not the ranking itself.

**The significance is that Nixara publicly confronts Erik without fear.**

This establishes the core relationship dynamic:

| Character | Core Trait |
|-----------|------------|
| Erik Douglas | Control |
| Nixara Bloodmoon | Passion |

**This contrast becomes one of the defining foundations of the Douglas-Bloodmoon family.**
