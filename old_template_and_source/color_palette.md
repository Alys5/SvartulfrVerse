# Modern World: Color Palette

Concrete HEX values for the Modern Dynasty visual aesthetics.

## Primary Colors
- **Gold:** `#D4AF37`
- **Amber:** `#FFBF00`
- **Walnut:** `#3E2723`
- **Black:** `#0A0A0A`

## Secondary Colors
- **Charcoal:** `#333333`
- **Gunmetal:** `#2A3439`
- **Warm Bronze:** `#CD7F32`

## Accent Colors
- **Crimson:** `#DC143C` (Used sparingly for warnings, blood, or high-stakes elements)
- **Ice Blue:** `#A5F2F3` (Used for digital screens, sterile tech, or corporate coldness)
