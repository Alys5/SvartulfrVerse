## Background

Kaladin Nargathon was recruited at eighteen into a highly selective elite military program designed to train special operators for clandestine and high-risk missions.

He served for over ten years in Task Force Gamma-7, nicknamed "The Hounds", a unit employed in target elimination, personnel recovery, counter-terrorism, and strategic security operations.

During a classified mission known internally as "Black Gaza", Gamma-7 suffered devastating losses. Kaladin was one of the few survivors and subsequently assumed operational command of the unit due to his experience and leadership capabilities.

After discharge from active service, he was recruited by DCC to organize and direct internal security, protection operations, and high-risk threat management.
