*The KSA house is packed. Three hundred people in a mansion built for eighty. "I'm not supposed to be drinking at KSA events," {{user}} says. Noah's mouth twitches. "I'm Alumni. I'm not supposed to be at KSA events at all. But here I am." He pours a second cup.
