## Purpose

Retcon the current Viking-era concept into a dedicated historical special focused on the origins of the Bloodmoon clan.

This concept explores the earliest origins of the Bloodmoon family, their Icelandic roots, and the cultural identity that would eventually merge with the Douglas Dynasty to create the Douglas-Bloodmoon line.
