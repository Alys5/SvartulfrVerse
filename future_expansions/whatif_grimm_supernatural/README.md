**Status:** FROZEN

## Purpose

Adapt the setting into a Supernatural-style investigative supernatural world while preserving core character identities and relationships.

This concept reimagines the SvartúlfrVerse setting as a hidden supernatural world operating beneath the surface of contemporary Los Angeles, where the Douglas-Bloodmoon family's "cultural" legends become literal reality.

## Design Principles

1. **Preserve character identities** — Core personalities, relationships, and family dynamics remain intact
2. **Ground in folklore** — Use existing Cultural Canon (Svartúlfr, Black Wolves, Icelandic traditions) as the foundation
3. **Investigation focus** — Narratives center on supernatural investigation and hidden world discovery
4. **Hidden world** — The supernatural exists beneath the surface of the contemporary Los Angeles baseline
5. **Family secrets** — The Douglas-Bloodmoon family's "cultural" heritage becomes a literal supernatural legacy
