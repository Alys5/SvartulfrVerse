**Status:** FROZEN

## Type

## Purpose

Retcon the current Regency-era concept into a dedicated historical special focused on the foundation of the Douglas Commercial Coalition.

This concept explores the earliest documented origins of the Douglas family's commercial legacy, predating the American colonial period and establishing the foundational narrative of the Douglas Dynasty's economic power.
