## Dynasty Overview

**Canon Layer:** [ACTIVE]  
**Source:** database/families/F_Douglas_Bloodmoon.md  
**Authority:** Family Authority (ADR-002)  
**Entry ID:** LB-HC-001-001

The **Douglas-Bloodmoon Dynasty** is a contemporary Los Angeles-based corporate dynasty formed through the union of the Douglas family (English origin, established American merchants since the 1700s) and the Bloodmoon family (Icelandic origin, first American-born generation in 1948). The dynasty operates through the **Douglas Commerce Company (DCC)**, a corporate empire with security, logistics, and commercial divisions.

The family resides primarily at the **Douglas Estate** in Beverly Hills, California. Family governance operates through informal **Sunday lunch meetings** at the Formal Dining Hall, which function as the family's internal government.

## Dynastic Origins — Bloodmoon Line

**Authority:** Family Authority (ADR-002)

The Bloodmoon family traces its American roots to **Icelandic immigration during the 1930s-1940s**. The first American-born Bloodmoon was **Wulfnic Bloodmoon (born 1948)**, who grew up straddling two worlds: the Old World traditions of his parents' homeland and the emerging American postwar prosperity.

The Bloodmoon cultural heritage includes the **Svartúlfr (Black Wolf)** tradition — an ancestral clan identity from Iceland representing values of loyalty, protection, and endurance. This is preserved as **Cultural Canon** (not supernatural) and maintained by Wulfnic as the family's cultural custodian.

## The Douglas-Bloodmoon Union

**Canon Layer:** [ACTIVE]  
**Source:** database/families/F_Marriages.md, database/characters/C_Erik_Douglas.md, database/characters/C_Nixara_Bloodmoon.md  
**Authority:** Family Authority (ADR-002), Character Authority (ADR-003)  
**Entry ID:** LB-HC-001-004

The dynastic union was formed through the marriage of **Erik Douglas (born 1970)** and **Nixara Bloodmoon (born 1975, died 2005)**. Their meeting in 1994 — when Nixara stormed the UCLA Bruins locker room to confront Erik in Icelandic — is the foundational family story.

Nixara died in 2005 during childbirth, delivering the twins Jasper and Alyssa. Her death is the foundational family trauma that shapes every member of the dynasty. Erik has never recovered from the loss and channels his grief into overprotective control of the remaining family.

The union produced four heirs, all carrying the hyphenated surname **Douglas-Bloodmoon**:
- Malachia (born 1996)
- Noah (born 1999)
- Jasper (born 2005)
- Alyssa (born 2005)
