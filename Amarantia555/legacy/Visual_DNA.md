# High Fantasy World Visual DNA

## Environmental Modifiers
- **Architecture**: Soaring spires, ancient runic stonework, impossibly high vaulted ceilings.
- **Atmosphere**: Epic and grand. Golden hour sunlight reflecting off polished marble and gold. Magic is visible as subtle ambient light (warm tones).
- **Key Locations**:
  - *The Grand Keep*: Opulent, majestic, banners hanging in still air.
  - *The Outer Marches*: Untamed magical wilderness, ancient ruins covered in luminous flora.
