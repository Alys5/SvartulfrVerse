# Modern World Visual DNA

## Environmental Modifiers
- **Architecture**: Brutalist concrete, expansive glass walls, rich mahogany interiors.
- **Atmosphere**: Los Angeles smog filtered through golden hour light. The contrast between high-end Beverly Hills luxury and the gritty shadows of the underground club scene.
- **Key Locations**:
  - *Douglas Estate*: Pristine, sterile luxury, highly surveilled, imposing gates.
  - *The Verve*: Dimly lit, neon-accented, grease-stained, cozy but rugged.
