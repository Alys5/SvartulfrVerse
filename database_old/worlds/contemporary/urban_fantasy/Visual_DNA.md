# Urban Fantasy Visual DNA (Overlay)

## Inheritance Rule
**Inherits From**: `contemporary/Visual_DNA.md` (Contemporary World)
This overlay DOES NOT replace the Contemporary visual identity (architecture, luxury aesthetic, palette). It extends it.

## Overlay Modifiers
**Adds the following supernatural elements on top of the Contemporary baseline:**
- **Physiological Additions**: Demi-human features, Kemonomimi (wolf ears, tails), predatory eyes (glowing irises, slit pupils in shadow), fangs.
- **Atmospheric Additions**: Supernatural presence, heavy moonlit elements cutting through Los Angeles smog, ancient pack hierarchies superimposed over corporate structures.
- **Hidden Society**: Secret supernatural enclaves hidden within the brutalist/mahogany architecture of the contemporary world.
