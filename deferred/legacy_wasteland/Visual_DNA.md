# Wasteland Visual DNA

## Environmental Modifiers
- **Architecture**: Crumbling skyscrapers, rusted industrial ruins, makeshift shelters from debris.
- **Atmosphere**: Post-apocalyptic desolation, dust storms, harsh sunlight, abandoned vehicles and debris scattered across cracked earth.
- **Key Locations**:
  - *Ruined Cities*: Collapsed skyscrapers, looted storefronts, danger lurking in shadows.
  - *Scavenger Camps**: Impermanent shelters built from scrap, communal fires, desperate communities.
  - *Industrial Wastelands*: Rusted factories, contaminated zones, valuable salvage buried under tons of debris.
