# Canon Note — Valeria, Jasper, Alyssa & Myrick

## Status

Approved Canon Concept (Pending Full Character Refactor)

---

## Timeline

Approximately one year before the birth of Alyssa and Myrick, Valeria gave birth to Jasper.

At the time:

* Erik Douglas was approximately 34 years old.
* Valeria was approximately 18–19 years old.
* Jasper was her firstborn child.

Shortly afterward, Nixara gave birth to Alyssa and Myrick but died during childbirth.

---

## The Shared Litter Concept

Following Nixara's death, Valeria became the only maternal figure physically present during the infants' earliest years.

Because she was already nursing Jasper, she also nursed Alyssa and Myrick.

As a result:

* Jasper, Alyssa, and Myrick were raised together from infancy.
* They shared the same nursery.
* They shared the same caregivers.
* They were treated by Wulfnic as members of the same "litter."

While Jasper is biologically a half-brother, the family rarely makes that distinction.

Within Douglas-Bloodmoon family culture:

> Biology matters.
>
> Family matters more.

---

## Emotional Dynamics

### Alyssa

Alyssa remembers Valeria as one of the closest things she ever had to a mother.

She remains largely unaware of the deeper resentment Valeria carries.

---

### Myrick

Myrick is far more skeptical.

He recognizes Valeria's political nature and often questions her motives.

---

### Jasper

Jasper occupies a unique position.

He understands both sides.

He knows Valeria genuinely cared for all three children.

He also understands the bitterness and jealousy she carries toward Nixara's memory.

Because of this, Jasper often acts as an informal buffer between Valeria and the twins.

---

## Valeria's Core Tragedy

Valeria raised the children.

Nixara became the legend.

She spent years building the family while living beneath the shadow of a woman who was no longer alive.

Her resentment is directed less toward Alyssa herself and more toward what Alyssa represents:

* Nixara's legacy.
* Erik's greatest love.
* The life Valeria could never replace.

---

## Characterization Rule

Valeria is NOT a stereotypical evil stepmother.

She should never be portrayed as openly abusive or cartoonishly cruel.

Instead:

* She is elegant.
* Calculating.
* Socially brilliant.
* Excessively attentive.
* Perfectly controlled.

Her affection may be genuine.

Her resentment may also be genuine.

These two truths coexist.

---

## Narrative Function

This concept exists to:

1. Preserve Jasper as a core Douglas sibling.
2. Allow Alyssa and Myrick to function as true twins.
3. Strengthen the family-centered themes of the SvartúlfrVerse.
4. Add nuance and tragedy to Valeria's role.
5. Reinforce the Bloodmoon concept of a shared litter over strict biological distinction.
