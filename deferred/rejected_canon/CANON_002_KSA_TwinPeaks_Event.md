## ESTABLISHED FACTS

**CONFIRMED:**

**Event Name:** Miss Twin Peaks KSA

**Host Organization:** Kappa Sigma Alpha fraternity

**Event Type:** Goliardic contest, defined by Alyssa as "stupido e leggendario"

**Duration of Tradition:** Sacred event for thirty years

**Contest Mechanics:**
* Fraternity athletes judge participants
* Judges described as "atleti con i bicipiti più grossi del cervello"
* Award given to the freshman with the most beautiful breasts according to their aesthetic standards
* Target participants: matricole, freshmen

**Historical Significance:**
* Erik met Nixara at this event approximately thirty years ago
* Nixara won the contest without wanting to participate
* Three fraternity members attempted to take Nixara upstairs during the event
* Nixara bit one attacker, leaving a scar visible for ten years
* Erik intervened and saved her

**Current Status:** The event is still running. Tonight's KSA party will host the Miss Twin Peaks contest, which Alyssa plans to attend.
