# Norse Mythic World Visual DNA

## Environmental Modifiers
- **Architecture**: Massive longhouses, dark timber, iron reinforcements, hearths taking center stage.
- **Atmosphere**: Bitter cold, howling winds, fjords. Ash falling like snow. Firelight creating stark contrasts against the creeping dark.
- **Key Locations**:
  - *The Iron Keep (Jarn-Gildi)*: Impregnable, built into the side of a mountain, echoing with the sounds of the forge.
  - *The Frozen Wastes*: Desolate, haunting, lit by auroras.
