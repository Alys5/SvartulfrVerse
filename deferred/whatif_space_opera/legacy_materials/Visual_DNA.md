# Scifi/Cyber World Visual DNA

## Environmental Modifiers
- **Architecture**: Monolithic brutalism, towering spires of black metal and glass, labyrinthine under-levels.
- **Atmosphere**: Oppressive megacity sprawl. Rain-slicked streets. While neon exists, it is subdued, often replaced by harsh clinical white/blue lighting in corporate sectors and deep amber/orange smog in the lower levels.
- **Key Locations**:
  - *Bloodmoon Industries Spire*: High above the smog, pristine, sterile, absolute control.
  - *The Sprawl/Undertrade*: Gritty, shadowed, lit by sparks and failing holoscreens.
