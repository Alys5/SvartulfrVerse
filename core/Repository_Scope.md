# Repository Scope

## Supported Scope

### Geography
- Los Angeles (primary setting)

### Population
- Only Human (no supernatural, no factions, no paranormal)

### Timeline
- Contemporary (2020s-2030s)

### Dynastic Focus
- Los Angeles Dynasty
  - Bloodmoon Dynasty (Wulfnic Bloodmoon)
  - Douglas Dynasty (Erik Douglas)
  - Douglas-Bloodmoon Union (Nixara Bloodmoon + Erik Douglas)
  - Core Line: Malachia, Noah, Jasper, Alyssa Douglas-Bloodmoon

## Explicitly Out of Scope (Archived)

### World Systems
- Urban Fantasy
- Cyber
- Wasteland
- Fantasy
- Norse Mythic
- Regency

### Narrative Systems
- Overlay Systems
- Pack Systems
- Faction Systems
- Caste Systems

### Supernatural Elements
- Paranormal abilities
- Immortality mechanics
- Transformation rules
- Magic systems
- Divine intervention

## Reintroduction Policy

No archived system may be reintroduced until:

1. Complete canonical audit performed
2. Historical variations documented
3. Contemporary Los Angeles Dynasty stability confirmed
4. Explicit architecture review completed
5. Formal decision recorded in ADR
6. Integration plan approved

## Rationale

Focus on contemporary Only Human Los Angeles Dynasty allows:

- Deep canonical stability
- Realistic character timelines
- Clear family authority boundaries
- Elimination of system contradictions
- Foundation for future expansion

Expansion to other worlds / systems follows only after this foundation is bulletproof.
