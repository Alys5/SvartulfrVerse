# Repository Scope

## Supported Content

The SvartúlfrVerse repository currently supports only the following content types:

### Only Human
- Human characters only
- No supernatural elements
- No fantasy races
- No cyber-enhancements
- No magical abilities
- No non-human species

### Contemporary
- Modern real-world setting
- Current technology level
- Real-world geography and locations
- Contemporary social structures
- Present-day cultural norms

### Los Angeles Dynasty
- Bloodmoon Dynasty (Wulfnic Bloodmoon)
- Douglas Dynasty (Erik Douglas)
- Douglas-Bloodmoon Line (Nixara Bloodmoon + Erik Douglas union)
- First-generation heirs:
  - Malachia Douglas-Bloodmoon
  - Noah Douglas-Bloodmoon
  - Jasper Douglas-Bloodmoon
  - Alyssa Douglas-Bloodmoon

## Not Supported Content

The following content types are explicitly out of scope for the current repository:

### Urban Fantasy
- Supernatural elements in modern settings
- Hidden magic systems
- Urban supernatural creatures
- Modern fantasy worlds

### Cyber
- Cyberpunk settings
- Advanced technology beyond contemporary levels
- Cyber-enhancements
- Dystopian future worlds
- AI entities as characters

### Wasteland
- Post-apocalyptic settings
- Scavenging societies
- Resource-scarce worlds
- Survival-focused environments

### Fantasy
- Medieval fantasy settings
- High magic worlds
- Fantasy creatures and races
- Alternate physics or natural laws

### Norse Mythic
- Norse mythology settings
- Asgardian realms
- Mythological creatures
- God-like entities
- Historical Viking settings with mythological elements

### Regency
- Historical period settings
- Regency-era social structures
- Historical accuracy constraints
- Period-specific cultural norms

### Overlay Systems
- Multi-layered reality systems
- Dimensional overlays
- Parallel world interactions
- Reality-bending mechanics

### Pack Systems
- Werewolf pack dynamics
- Supernatural hierarchy systems
- Pack-based social structures
- Alpha/beta/omega dynamics

### Supernatural Systems
- Vampire societies
- Witch covens
- Magic systems
- Supernatural power structures

## Scope Expansion Policy

Content types listed as "Not Supported" may be reconsidered for future reintroduction only after:

1. Completion of current scope validation (Only Human, Contemporary, LA Dynasty)
2. Stable canonical implementation of current scope
3. Architectural review for expansion impact
4. Canon recovery workflow completion for proposed content
5. Explicit ADR documenting scope expansion decision

No scope expansion occurs without formal documentation and validation.

## Scope Validation

Any character, world, or experience introduced to the repository must:

1. Align with "Only Human" constraint
2. Align with "Contemporary" setting
3. Align with "Los Angeles Dynasty" focus
4. Pass LA_OnlyHuman_Academic_Timeline validation
5. Receive family engine validation for relationships

Content that violates scope constraints will be rejected or require scope expansion approval through ADR process.

## Rationale

The limited scope enables:

- Focused canonical stabilization
- Prevention of scope creep and canonical drift
- Validation of architectural principles
- Controlled expansion from stable foundation
- Clear canonical boundaries

Broad scope without validated foundation was a primary cause of canonical drift in the historical repository.
